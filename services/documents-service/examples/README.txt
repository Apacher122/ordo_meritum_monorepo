Your resumes, cover letters, match summaries, and review points will be added here.

- match_summaries/
    - All the files here will attempt to show you how you stack up to the job you're applying for based on your resume.

- guiding_answers/
    - Insights on different job's your applying for like:
        - what you should consider when applying to that position
        - skills you should probably brush up on
        - what might interest you about the Company
        - general information about the company (i.e., company culture, work styles, etc.)

You may ignore the .aux, .log, and .out files. They're just stuff generated when LaTeX compiles into PDF.
